Robots and the Automation of Everyday Life


For almost one hundred years, we’ve told wild stories about robots. The Robot Age has always been almost upon us, at once threatening our lives and our livelihoods and promising a world of machine intelligence and machine labor.

Each week, I update this website with the list of the latest “robot news.” These are the stories currently in circulation about the world-to-come and the automation of everyday life.
